About StockSim

Welcome to StockSim, a dynamic platform designed to simulate the stock market and provide users with a risk-free environment to practice trading strategies, learn about market trends, and improve their trading skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and realistic trading simulation to help both new and experienced traders refine their trading tactics and decisions without financial risk.

Privacy Statement

We respect your privacy. StockSim is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of all participants using our platform:

  • Data Protection: We ensure that all personal information is securely managed and kept confidential. The only data stored is name, email id and password.
  • User Anonymity: We prioritize user anonymity in trading activities, with no real money involved and no personal trading data shared with any third parties.
